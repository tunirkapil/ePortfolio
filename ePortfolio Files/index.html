Identity

Diversity. Adventurous. Curious. That’s me in a nutshell. I’m currently a third year student whose discovering his passion in a Combined Major in Business and Computer Science at the University of British Columbia. It’s been a fun challenge that’s constantly allowed me to switch from a technical to a business mindset.

Business

Like many children’s dreams, I wanted to be the CEO of my own company when I grew up. I came to realize that for any business to thrive against its competitors, those in charge need to have a solid understanding of the core fundamentals of the entire business world. It is through the various experiences both inside and out of class that’s has allowed me to develop useable skills that I’m ready to put to test upon entering the workforce.

Computer Science

Headphones? Check. Computer? Check. Game? Check. This checklist hasn’t changed for me to this day. From playing my first video game till now, I’ve always been curious how games were made. It was through this obsession that I’ve enjoyed learning various software development languages. I’m constantly looking for opportunities to learn more.

Hobbies

Beside spending a majority of my time learning new skills, I’ve been lucky enough to travel the world and have grown to love it. Spanning a cultural intelligence of six countries (United Arab Emirates, Egypt, Russia, France, South Africa, Canada and India) has allowed me to develop a craving to learn about new cultures that I have not yet been exposed to. I’ve also enjoyed challenging myself physically. Anything that gets my heart rate going, is something I’m willing to try.
